Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Proteticalc stores only the necessary information for system operation, such as user registration and calculation history. No sensitive or financial data is collected.

2. Privacy and Security

The data provided is used exclusively for the operation of the application. There is no integration with third-party APIs or services.

3. User Rights

All data is personal, non-transferable, and securely stored. Proteticalc does not share information with third parties under any circumstances.

4. Sharing Information

When logged in, users have the ability to share specific saved history data with third parties by sending a particular calculation if they wish to do so.

5. Policy Changes

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data stored in the app. To do so, simply contact support.

6. Data Collection

The creator reserves the right to modify this policy, notifying users of relevant changes.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at proteticalc@gmail.com.
