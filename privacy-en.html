Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 28, 2025

The Proteticalc application does not collect or store any personal user data. The app is intended solely to assist professionals with dental prosthesis measurements and performs all operations locally on the device.

1. Data Collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or store any type of personal, financial, or sensitive data. All information entered is processed locally and not shared with third parties.

2. Use of Information

The data input by the user is used only to perform internal calculations and is not stored in external servers or shared in any way.

3. Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, or contacts. Only local data entered by the user is used.

4. Ads and Analytics

The app does not include ads or use third-party analytics services.

5. Security

Since the app does not store or transmit personal data, the risk of unauthorized access to information is virtually zero.
